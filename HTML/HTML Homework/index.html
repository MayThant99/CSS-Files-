Welcome to Exotic Breaks

Treak yourself to the ultimate break by escaping to one of our exotic holiday distinations. Imagine lying on a white sandy
beach with coconut palms swaying in the breeze , cocktails at hand and the smell of spice in the air.

Exotic destinations such as the Caribbean are ever more accessible and just waiting to be discovered by you.

Exotic Breaks can also take you off the beaten track and lead you to discover all that the Far East and Asla have to
offer. Combine adventure with relaxation and head into the Indian Ocean and the islands of the Maldives.

Once the summer months, why not try a little adventure and fly to Kenya to experience a breathtaking safari . During July
and August the water level is lower and wild animals flock to water holes making the sighting of incredible beasts such as
lions , elephants and zebra even more likely for onlookers.

Whatever exotic break you are craving , the perfect solution is out there for you!
